Hamsters

Long Haired Syrian

Fun Facts:

  1. Syrians are one of the largest species of hamsters with an average length of 6 inches.
  2. Long Haired Syrian hamsters are nicknamed "Teddy Bear" hamsters because of their longer fur.

Roborovski

  1. They are the fastest dwarf hamster and can run over 100 miles in a single night.
  2. They are also the smallest dwarf hamster with an average length of 2.5 inches.
