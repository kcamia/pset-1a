Kittens

Nebelung

Fun Facts:

  1. In German, the word, Nebelung means 'creature of the mist'.
  2. These cats possess almond-shaped eyes that are widely spaced. The Nebelung's eye color is either vivid green or yellow-green.

Persian

Fun Facts:

  1. The Persian is one of the most popular cats in the United States.
  2. They were showcased in the world's first organized cat show in 1871 in London.
