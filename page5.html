Animal Organizations To Know

There are many organizations around the world that support and protect pets and wildlife. If you want to learn more or want to support these organizations, click their logo.

  • American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals

    • ASPCA is a non-profit organization based in New York. It was the first humane society to be established in North America. Their mission is to "provide effective means for the prevention of cruelty to animals throughout the United States."

  • Animal Welfare Institute

    • AWI is a non-profit organization founded in 1951 with the goal of reducing pain and fear inflicted on animals by humans. They work to address laws concerning cruel animal factories and animal testing.

  • Best Friends Animal Society

    • Best Friends Animal Society is an American non-profit animal welfare organization. They outreach nationwide with shelters and rescue groups to promote pet adoption, no-kill animal rescue, and spay-and-neuter practices.

  • Farm Animal Rights Movement

    • FARM is an international non-profit organization. They work to promote a vegan lifestyle and animal rights through public education. The organization operates ten national and international programs from its headquarters in Maryland.

  • Farm Sanctuary

    • Farm Sanctuary is an American farmed animal protection organization. They promote laws and policies that support animal welfare, animal protection, and veganism through rescue, education, and advocacy.
