Corgi

Fun Facts:

  1. According to Welsh legend,the corgi is an enchanted dog favored by fairies and elves.
  2. Queen Elizabeth II has had more than 30 corgis in her lifetime.

Maltese

  1. The Maltese is one of the oldest-known breeds of dogs, and is said to be over 2800 years old.
  2. They were specially bred by Roman emperors to have a white coat. The color white was sacred to the Romans, who wanted their pets to exhibit an air of divinity.
